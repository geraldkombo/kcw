### The Mission
Kilimo Credit Web (KCW) is an **AI Agriculture Investment System** — not an application, but an autonomous intelligence layer that transforms agricultural data into investable assets. It spans all 5 tracks of the Mercy Corps AgriFin AI for Agriculture Hackathon: climate-smart agriculture (Track 1), last-mile service delivery (Track 2), financial inclusion (Track 3), data and decision support (Track 4, primary), and pest/disease/crop management (Track 5).
Kilimo Credit Web (KCW) is an **AI Agriculture Investment System** — not an application, but an autonomous intelligence layer that transforms agricultural data into investable assets. It solves the six fundamental risks that prevent capital from flowing to smallholder agriculture: **water uncertainty, solar underutilisation, soil degradation, poor seed quality, cooperative mismanagement, and market fragmentation.**

### The Six Risk Vectors We Eliminate

**1. Water Risk** — Derisked via real-time NASA POWER satellite precipitation data (PRECTOTCORR), root-zone soil moisture (GWETROOT), and precision irrigation scheduling. Farmers know exactly when and how much to irrigate. Solar-powered drip irrigation is recommended when equatorial solar makes it viable.

**2. Solar Risk** — Quantified and optimised via the equatorial solar advantage (+200% vs temperate zones). Every crop is matched to its optimal solar radiation range. Solar drying infrastructure is recommended when post-harvest losses exceed 20%.

**3. Nutrient/Soil Risk** — Assessed via real root-zone soil wetness (GWETROOT) and temperature (T2M) from NASA's MERRA-2 reanalysis. Soil quality is classified as good/moderate/poor with specific irrigation recommendations. Land suitability is computed for 7 crop types per field.

**4. Seed Risk** — Eliminated via germination rate prediction adjusted for local conditions. Poor-quality seed is detected before planting. Varieties are ranked by expected ROI: droughtguard_301 (1300% ROI) vs hybrid_525 vs local landrace. Seed investment is recommended as the highest-ROI first step.

**5. Cooperative Risk** — Solved via AI-driven cooperative governance scoring (A-D grades), milk pricing optimization (KES 10-15/litre upside), bulk feed procurement (10-25% savings), and digital ledger recommendations. Cooperative mismanagement is the largest silent tax on African agriculture — KCW eliminates it.

**6. Market Risk** — Eliminated via real-time best-price routing (spot vs contract farming), contract farming marketplace (5+ active buyers including Unga Limited, Kenya Nut Company, KTDA, Bidco), idle land development opportunity analysis, and feed cost optimization across suppliers.
